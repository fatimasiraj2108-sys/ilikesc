Why this portal exists

Across Pakistan, thousands of students from low-income families are academically capable but financially unable to continue school. Their potential goes unrealised not because of ability, but because of cost. This portal exists to close that gap — by making it simple to find funding and apply, without needing a computer expert or an agent to help.

About the programme

One shared goal, five clubs, one scholarship fund.

Success Club 2026 brings together five student clubs — Media, Business, Law, Social Sciences, and AI — around a single UN Sustainable Development Goal: SDG 4, Quality Education. Rather than running five separate projects, all five clubs are working toward one real, operational outcome: a merit-based scholarship programme for underprivileged students who have the potential to succeed academically but are held back by financial hardship.

This portal is the AI Club's contribution — the digital front door for the whole programme. It's where students discover which scholarships are open, understand whether they're likely to qualify, and submit a complete application without needing to visit an office in person.

The programme runs from 1 July to Mid August 2026, and is open to students in Karachi, Lahore, and Islamabad.

The five clubs, and what each one does

  • AI ClubDesigns and builds this portal, the eligibility checker, and the application system.
  • Media ClubSpreads the word to schools and communities, and tells applicants' stories with their permission.
  • Business ClubRaises the scholarship funds and manages the programme's budget.
  • Law ClubWrites the application terms, checks compliance, and protects applicants' data.
  • Social Sciences ClubResearches which communities need support most, and helps set fair eligibility criteria.

Our promise to applicants

Applying for a scholarship can feel intimidating. Here's what you can expect from us.

Your information stays private

Your details are only used to review your application. We don't share your information with anyone outside the review team.

Every application is reviewed

A real person reads every submission. The eligibility checker is only a quick estimate — it never makes the final decision on its own.

It's completely free

There is no fee to check your eligibility or to apply. If anyone asks you to pay to apply, it isn't us — please report it.
