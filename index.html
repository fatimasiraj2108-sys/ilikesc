Why this portal exists

Across Pakistan, thousands of students from low-income families are academically capable but financially unable to continue school. Their potential goes unrealised not because of ability, but because of cost. This portal exists to close that gap — by making it simple to find funding and apply, without needing a computer expert or an agent to help.

How it works

Three simple steps, from checking your eligibility to hearing back from us.

1

Check eligibility

Answer a few questions about your grade level, marks, and household income to see which scholarship tracks are likely to fit you.

2

Apply online

Fill in one application form with your details and share Google Drive links to your mark sheet, income proof, and ID — no attachments needed.

3

Hear back

You'll get an application ID as a receipt. Our reviewers use it to track your status and reach you if anything's missing.
