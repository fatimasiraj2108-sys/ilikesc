About us

Helping capable students get into the universities they've earned a place at.

Every year, thousands of students across Pakistan work hard, get the grades, and dream of studying at universities like LUMS, NUST, IBA, or GIKI — and every year, many of them don't make it, not because they aren't capable, but because their families can't afford the cost of getting there. This programme exists to close that gap.

We find students with real academic potential and financial need, walk them through a simple application, and connect approved students with scholarship funding that covers tuition so a lack of money never has to be the reason a capable student's education stops.

This portal is where that happens — where students discover which scholarships are open, understand whether they're likely to qualify, and submit a complete application without needing to visit an office in person. The current cycle runs from 1 July to Mid August 2026, open to students in Karachi, Lahore, and Islamabad.

Who's behind this

A small team from Success Club runs this programme end to end — finding students who need support, reviewing every application by hand, raising the funds that pay for tuition, and staying in touch with each scholar until they're settled at university.

Our promise to applicants

Applying for a scholarship can feel intimidating. Here's what you can expect from us.

Your information stays private

Your details are only used to review your application. We don't share your information with anyone outside the review team.

Every application is reviewed

A real person reads every submission. The eligibility checker is only a quick guide — it never makes the final decision on its own.

It's completely free

There is no fee to check your eligibility or to apply. If anyone asks you to pay to apply, it isn't us — please report it.
